When it won't roll up — how loudly do we explain why?

… ORDER BY AVG(value) DESC LIMIT 10
⚠ Won't roll up ↰ hover
TopN ordering can't be reproduced from the cube → source scan.

B1 · Badge + tooltip

Reason lives in the badge's hover tooltip. Closest to today, smallest footprint. Downside: the "why" is hidden until you hover — easy to miss, poor for teaching.

… ORDER BY AVG(value) DESC LIMIT 10
⚠ Won't roll up
TopN ordering can't be mapped onto the cube — falls back to a source scan.
Fix: order by a stored aggregate (count/sum/avg) or remove LIMIT to roll up.

B2 · Badge + inline reason & fix recommended

Badge always visible; when not eligible, a one-line reason + actionable fix appears right under it. Discoverable, teaches users to write rollup-friendly SQL. Collapses to nothing when eligible.

SELECT site, AVG(value) FROM downloads
WHERE … GROUP BY site HAVING AVG(value) > 5
⚠ Won't roll up
The underlined HAVING clause isn't supported by rollup.
Fix: filter in the panel or drop HAVING.

B3 · Inline editor diagnostics

Everything in B2 plus an amber squiggle on the exact clause that breaks rollup (Monaco marker), hover for the reason — a true "rollup linter." Most powerful & teaching, but more work and needs the backend to return the offending span.

My recommendation: ship B2 (always-visible badge + inline reason/fix) as the core, and treat B3 (inline squiggles) as an optional phase-2 enhancement layered on top. B1 alone is too quiet for something this useful. Click a card, or tell me your call in the terminal.
